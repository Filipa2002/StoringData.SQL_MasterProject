Here's a summary of the purpose of each trigger:

### 1. **`before_order_insert`**
   - **Purpose**: Automatically logs when a new order is created in the `Orders` table.
   - **Action**: Inserts a log entry into the `Logs` table whenever a new order is added, capturing the `OrderID` and `CustomerID`.

### 2. **`before_orderdetails_insert`**
   - **Purpose**: Updates the stock quantity when a new order detail is added in the `OrderDetails` table.
   - **Action**: Decreases the stock quantity in the `Products` table based on the ordered quantity.

### 3. **`before_orderdetails_update`**
   - **Purpose**: Updates the stock when the quantity in an existing order detail is modified.
   - **Action**: Adjusts the product stock in the `Products` table by reversing the previous quantity and applying the new quantity.

### 4. **`after_orderdetails_insert`**
   - **Purpose**: Updates the total amount of an order when new details are added.
   - **Action**: Recalculates the `TotalAmount` in the `Orders` table by summing the `Subtotal` of the `OrderDetails` associated with the order.

### 5. **`before_orderdetails_delete`**
   - **Purpose**: Reverts the stock quantity when an order detail is deleted.
   - **Action**: Increases the stock in the `Products` table based on the quantity that was removed from the order.

### 6. **`after_order_delete`**
   - **Purpose**: Logs when an order is deleted from the `Orders` table.
   - **Action**: Inserts a log entry into the `Logs` table with details of the deleted order (`OrderID` and `CustomerID`).

### 7. **`before_product_insert`**
   - **Purpose**: Prevents adding products with a negative stock quantity.
   - **Action**: Throws an error if the `StockQuantity` for a new product is less than 0.

### 8. **`after_product_update`**
   - **Purpose**: Logs price changes for products in the `Products` table.
   - **Action**: When a product's price is updated, it inserts a log entry into the `Logs` table, capturing the old and new prices.

### 9. **`after_log_insert`**
   - **Purpose**: Tracks when a new log entry is added.
   - **Action**: Optionally perform actions like notifications when a new log is created. This trigger doesn't have any immediate actions in this example, but can be customized for further tracking.
